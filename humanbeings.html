HUMAN BEINGS

Humans are one complex higher animals you can never understand. You can never please them, because if you do, you still fail at the end.

Even if you are faithful and loyal till the end, you will still be seen as a fool for being so loyal.

Parents fail children and verse-versa, friends fail each other, siblings also disappoint each other.

What can then be done? what is the right thing to do? should we get angry when all we think we do to gain trust still shatters?

Sincerely writing, you can never please any Human 100%.

If you are quiet and go your way, then you are so arrogant. If you are free-minded and relate with everyone, then you are so disorganised. There is a title for everything.

The best to do is to do what you think is right, do what pleases God. Be free-minded towards people without keeping grudges. Love whole heartedly without pretense. Lastly, don't do anything to gain favour or for people to love you. Instead do what God will be proud of you.
