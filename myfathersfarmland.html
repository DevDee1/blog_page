EXCERPT FROM "MY FATHER'S FARMLAND"

Live your life like you don't own it. Always remember God created you, hence He is the one who own the copyright to your life

Live your life pleasing God in deeds and with your talents, because He was the one who gave you the gifts in the first place

Let not the love of money, future and power push you away from God, because when your time is up on earth, you will leave all that behind and that God you have pushed far away from you is the same God you will have to face without a choice

Nothing pays than serving God with your skills and talent

In all that you do, in all that you have, and in all position that you find yourself, do everything to love the Lord your God, obey His commandments and live every day of your life in christ, bearing in mind that after death is judgement.
